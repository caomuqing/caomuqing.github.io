We develop a fully actuated hexa-rotor aerial manipulator, and apply diffusion based trajectory generation algorithm to do autonomous light bulb installation and many other tasks! The demonstration are collected using handhold universal manipulator interface (UMI)!

Paper: UMI-on-Air: <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.02614">Embodiment-Aware Guidance for Embodiment-Agnostic Visuomotor Policies</a>, in collaboration with Prof. Guanya Shi (CMU), Prof. Shuran Song (Stanford)
